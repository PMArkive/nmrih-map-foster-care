nmo_southpole Objectives List (How I originally intended them, based on the latest version.)

1. Get out of the cafeteria.

2. Find your way to the station’s bridge.

3. Contact the circling helicopter in the radio room.

4. Find and secure two ingredients for the final vaccine.

5. Go to the underground area.

6. Restore backup power to the underground.

7. Load the vaccine ingredients onto the payload.

8. Hold out in the dome until rescue arrives!
